Our Mission Statement

La Familia strives to encourage young Latinx indivials to succeed academically, become leaders of their communities and build authentic relationships with others. Our core values of academic excellence, community service, and social networking highlight the impact we can make within ourselves and throughout our community. La Familia recognizes we are “Bajo el Mismo Sol” or “Under the Same Sun.”
